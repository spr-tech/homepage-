Visit ILORIN

The capital city of Kwara State located in the Western region of Nigeria, the city is considered a Yoruba city by all historical and sociological standards.

Top three places to visit in ILORIN

Ilorin Emirate Durbar

This is the social dispaly of tradition culture skills, it is celebrated after the Muslim festivals of Eid al-Fitr and Eid al-Adha, typically on the third day of Eid al-Adha. It's an annual event that showcases the rich cultural heritage and unity of the Ilorin Emirate.

University of Ilorin

UNILORIN is a federal government-owned public research university in Ilorin, Kwara State, Nigeria. The university's main campus sits on an expansive area of land, about 5,000 hectares in the ancient city of Ilorin, making it the largest university in Nigeria.

Ilorin Emirate Mall

Step into a realm of luxury and lifestyle at Ilorin Emirate Mall Situated in the bustling city of Ilorin.Immerse yourself in a realm of sophistication as you explore the synergy of fashion, gastronomy, and leisure.
